# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-19_

Pocket Buddha is designed to respect your privacy. This document explains what data the app handles and how.

## The short version

- **We have no servers.** Pocket Buddha runs entirely on your device.
- **We do not collect, store, or transmit your conversations or personal information.**
- **We do not track you.** No analytics, no advertising, no telemetry.
- **Your Anthropic API key is stored encrypted on your device.**
- **When you send a message, it is transmitted directly from your device to Anthropic's servers,** using your own API key. Anthropic processes that message under their own privacy policy.

## What stays on your device

The following data lives only on your device and is never sent to us or any third party (except as noted):

- Your Anthropic API key (encrypted via your operating system's secure storage)
- All your conversations with Pocket Buddha
- Your bookmarked teachings
- Your practice streak history
- Your preferences (tradition, notification time)

If you delete the app or clear its storage, all of this data is destroyed.

## What is sent to Anthropic

When you send a message in the app, the following is sent **directly from your device to Anthropic's API:**

- The text of your message
- Recent conversation context (the back-and-forth needed for Buddha to respond coherently)
- The system prompt instructing the model how to respond
- Your Anthropic API key, for authentication

Anthropic's handling of this data is governed by Anthropic's privacy policy, which you should review separately at [https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy).

We have no access to and no control over Anthropic's processing.

## Notifications

If you enable Daily Wisdom notifications, the scheduling happens locally on your device. The notification content (a Buddhist quote and source) does not transmit any personal data.

## Children

Pocket Buddha is not directed at children under 13. The app discusses themes including suffering, death, and impermanence which require adult-level discernment. By using the app you confirm you are at least 13 years old; users under 18 should have parental consent.

## Crisis content

If you are in crisis, please use the Crisis Resources section of the app or contact emergency services in your country. Pocket Buddha is not a crisis intervention tool.

## Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update this document and the "Last updated" date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
